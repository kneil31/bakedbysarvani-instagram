## Caption Formula

### Structure
```
[Hook - max 125 chars] + [Optional humor/warmth] + [Soft CTA]

+
[Line break]
[Hashtags - 8-10 total]
```

### Hook Types (Pick One)

1. **The Pun**: Food/baking wordplay
   - "This cake really knows how to make an entrance"

2. **The Moment**: Celebrate the occasion
   - "When the cake gets more attention than the birthday kid"

3. **The Reaction**: Anticipate viewer response
   - "POV: You just saw the wings move"

4. **The Flex** (humble): Subtle pride in work
   - "Not to brag, but this one made ME gasp"

### Length Guidelines
- **Main caption**: 1-2 sentences (under 150 chars ideal)
- **Total with hashtags**: Under 300 chars for clean look
- Instagram shows ~125 chars before "...more"

## Quick Reference: Caption Request Format

When asking for a new caption, provide:
1. **Cake type**: (e.g., kinetic butterfly, gender reveal, birthday)
2. **Occasion**: (birthday, baby shower, etc.)
3. **Any special details**: (theme, colors, who it's for)
4. **Mood**: (fun, elegant, playful, heartfelt)

Example request:
> "Caption for a pink and gold princess birthday cake for a 5-year-old"

## Engagement Tips

### Best Posting Times for DFW Telugu Audience
- **Weekdays**: 7-9 PM (after work/dinner)
- **Weekends**: 10 AM - 12 PM (brunch scroll time)
- **Sunday evenings**: High engagement (planning week ahead)

### Story Ideas
- Behind-the-scenes cake assembly
- "Before vs After" decoration
- Cake cutting moment (repost from clients)
- Polls: "Which design next?"

### Reels/Video Captions
- Keep even shorter (under 100 chars)
- Front-load the hook
- Example: "Wait for it... those wings MOVE"
